About Memeverse 3.0

🎭

The Ultimate Meme Battle

Memeverse 3.0 is the most creative and hilarious meme competition designed exclusively for 2nd and 3rd-year students from all engineering departments. It's where creativity meets humor, and where the best memes rise to legendary status!

Organized at Adhiparasakthi Engineering College, this competition celebrates the art of meme-making and digital creativity across all engineering disciplines.

Why Participate?

  • Showcase your creativity
  • Win exciting prizes
  • Connect with fellow memers
  • Get recognition

Rules & Guidelines

Eligibility

  • Open to 2nd & 3rd year students from all departments
  • Individual participation only
  • One entry per participant

Content Rules

  • Original content only
  • No offensive or inappropriate content
  • Must relate to chosen theme

Submission

  • Submit before deadline
  • High-quality image format
  • Include team/individual details

Judging

  • Creativity & originality
  • Humor & relatability
  • Theme adherence
